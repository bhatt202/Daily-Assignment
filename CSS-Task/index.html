Lion

The lion is a large cat of the genus Panthera native to Africa and India. It has a muscular, broad-chested body; short, rounded head; round ears; and a hairy tuft at the end of its tail.

Tiger

The tiger is the largest living cat species and a member of the genus Panthera.

Lepord

The leopard is one of the five extant species in the genus Panthera, a member of the cat family, Felidae.

Cheetah

Fastest mammal on land, the cheetah can reach speeds of 60 or perhaps even 70 miles (97 or 113 kilometers) an hour over short distances.

Dove

Columbidae is a bird family consisting of doves and pigeons. It is the only family in the order Columbiformes.

Parrot

Parrots, also known as psittacines, are birds of the roughly 398 species in 92 genera comprising the order Psittaciformes, found mostly in tropical and subtropical regions.
